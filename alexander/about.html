About Us

Alexander's Uniforms is a family owned and operated uniform company. In business since 1945, our three retail locations offer Southeastern New England's largest in store selections. Our on-site sales program works with hospitals, nursing homes, and medical office buildings from Maine through Pennsylvania.

Our staff has over 100 years of combined experience, allowing us to outfit you, your business, or school in any healthcare, hospitality, chefwear, industrial, restaurant or professional apparel.

Let decades of customer satisfaction work for you and call us toll free for any of your uniform needs.
